I believe in God. But not the boxed version that religions fight wars over.

## God Is Energy

I see God as **energy, frequency, and vibration.** The more aligned I am with that energy, the better life flows. Simple as that.

Tesla said it best — *"Think in terms of energy, frequency and vibration."* I arrived at the same place on my own. That felt like confirmation.

## Science Doesn't Disprove God

Fundamentalist atheists say science proves God doesn't exist. That's a logical error.

Science has mapped roughly **1% of the universe.** You cannot look at 1% and declare the other 99% empty. That's not science — that's faith in absence.

The greatest scientists — Einstein, Tesla, Bohr — all pointed at something *beyond* the data. Real science is humble.

## Evolution AND God

Am I a creationist or an evolutionist?

**Yes.**

Evolution almost certainly happened. But God was the **impulse that split the first cell.** Evolution is *how* God created — not the alternative to it.

From that one impulse — plants, animals, humans. All of it.

## No Box. No Label.

I learn from every religion. I belong to none. I take what resonates and leave the rest.

My relationship with God is mine. No middleman needed.
