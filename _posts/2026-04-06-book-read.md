## 🎙️ ADHD Chatter Podcast — Alex Partridge

> **Who is he?** Founded LADBible & UNILAD at 21. Diagnosed with ADHD at 34. Hosts the #1 ADHD podcast globally. 700,000+ monthly listeners. Sunday Times Bestselling author.

---

### 📖 Book 1 — Now It All Makes Sense *(Jan 2025)*
**Subtitle:** How An ADHD Diagnosis Brought Clarity To My Life

- His origin story — from LADBible founder → alcoholism → ADHD diagnosis at 34
- Distills the best insights from 100+ podcast expert interviews into one book
- Covers: finances, parenting, masking, hyper-focus, emotional dysregulation, RSD flare-ups, relationships
- Short, raw, honest — written *for* an ADHD brain
- **Status:** Sunday Times Instant Bestseller ⭐

> 💬 *Edward Hallowell MD: "One of the most complete and moving accounts I've ever read."*

---

### 📖 Book 2 — Why Does Everybody Hate Me? *(2025)*
**Subtitle:** Living and Loving with Rejection Sensitivity Dysphoria

- The **only mainstream book dedicated entirely to RSD**
- RSD = the intense emotional pain from perceived or real rejection — affects almost everyone with ADHD
- By age 12, an ADHD child has received 20,000 more negative messages than a neurotypical child. This book unpacks that wound.
- Covers: people-pleasing, imposter syndrome, relationships, boundaries, self-belief
- Draws on Alex's own RSD journey + world's top ADHD experts
- **Status:** #1 New Release (ADHD category)

> 💬 *William Dodson MD: "Thorough, articulate, beautifully written and validating for people with RSD."*

> 💬 *Dr Alex George: "He takes away the stigma and shame, replacing them with compassion and hope."*

**What is RSD? — Quick explainer:**
| RSD Trigger | What your brain tells you |
|---|---|
| Boss says "can we chat?" | "I'm being fired" |
| Friend sends 👍 emoji | "They hate me" |
| Someone leaves a movie early | "They hated my taste" |
| No reply to a message | "They're ignoring me forever" |

*None of these are rational. RSD makes them feel like facts.*

## 🧠 The Core ADHD Reading Stack

### 1. ADHD 2.0 — *Ned Hallowell & John Ratey*
- **Start here.** Both authors have ADHD themselves.
- Modern science + strengths-based approach.
- They've been on Huberman Lab, countless YouTube interviews.
- **Best for:** Understanding *why* your brain works the way it does.

---

### 2. Taking Charge of Adult ADHD — *Russell Barkley*
- The most science-backed book on adult ADHD.
- Barkley has a massive YouTube channel — his lectures are basically this book, free.
- **Best for:** Practical strategies, executive function, relationships, work.

---

### 3. Atomic Habits — *James Clear*
- Not ADHD-specific — but written *for* an ADHD brain without knowing it.
- Systems > willpower. Tiny changes. Identity-based change.
- **Best for:** Actually building habits that stick.

---

### 4. Driven to Distraction — *Hallowell & Ratey*
- The 1994 classic. Still the most-cited ADHD book ever.
- Read *after* ADHD 2.0 — same authors, older but foundational.
- **Best for:** Deep understanding of ADHD across the lifespan.

---

### 5. How to ADHD — *Jessica McCabe*
- Creator of the *How to ADHD* YouTube channel.
- Lived experience + science. Most relatable voice in the space.
- **Best for:** Practical, bite-sized, zero shame.

---

### 6. Spark — *John Ratey*
- Exercise and the ADHD brain. Science of why movement = medication.
- Ratey is co-author of Driven to Distraction.
- **Best for:** If you want to understand the biology behind why walking helps.
